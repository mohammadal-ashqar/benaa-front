Welcome to Benaa Society

Be among the companions of this verse

"Indeed, they used to hasten to good deeds."

Benaa association plays a leading role in charitable work and contributes to alleviating the suffering of the Palestinian citizens, emphasizing the provision of full support to poor families and orphans and the advancement of society as a whole by promoting community and awareness issues, and community service.

Learn More

Our Goal

Benaa association is a non-profit charitable civil society organization that aims to provide comprehensive exceptional services to targeted groups through a package of innovative projects, committed to the principles of human rights and transparency in service delivery.

  • Caring for orphans and needy families in Gaza
  • Supporting community activities by the local community.
  • Activate the recreational activities of the organization.
  • Prioritizing women and children's culture and well-being for social development.

Benaa Projects

All Projects

Food parcels

Benaa Association distributed integrated food parcels by providing Ramadan food baskets to needy families in the region, through a generous donation from the benefactors.

Bag & school uniform

Benaa Association distribution of school bags, supplies, and uniforms to needy students in the Gaza Strip to improve the educational situation.

Wheat flour aid

The Binaa Association distributed local wheat flour to needy families to support food security as part of the "Supporting the Wheat Value Chain" project.

Fasting breakfast

Benaa Association distributed Iftar boxes to Palestinians during Ramadan, including milk, water, and dates to worshipers in mosques and passers-by in Khan Younis.

Our history

Bina Society was founded in 2013 as a non-profit civil society organization operating under the Palestinian Charitable Societies and Non-Governmental Organizations Law No. (1) of 2000, licensed by the Ministry of Interior, with a mission to provide care and development services to the community.

Vision

The leading and distinguished association in providing charitable and developmental services to marginalized groups in Palestine.

Association achievements

  • Providing psychological and social support for marginalized groups through periodic home visits.
  • Providing a detailed database that specializes in poor families and orphaned families.
  • Conducting medical days that benefited large numbers of poor and needy families.
  • Providing in-kind assistance to marginalized and vulnerable groups in Palestinian society, especially orphans and low-income families.
